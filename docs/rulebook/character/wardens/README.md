# Wardens

Wardens are the sacred guardians of the people, each archetype holds an important role in their society.

A Warden carries many tools, which they use sparingly and only against specific threats.

Each Warden carries a **Relic** which is the symbol of their role

> This Sacred Relic have been handed down through the ages. In the Hands of the Warden the Sacred Relic enhances their actions by granting a bonus dice, it can also be **Invoked** by spending 3 Spirit to gain its **Boon**.

They wield **Talismans** that aid them and at times save their lives.

> All Talismans have associated abilities that can be **Invoked** by spending one Spirit. They can be **Shattered** to gain additional effects or gain an automatic Dramatic Success.

They are empowered by **Rites** that show their skill and wisdom.

> Sacred Rites are unique to each Warden and is based on their domain. The Warden can spend 3 point of Spirit to **Invoke** their Rite. _If the player has no more Spirit they can perform their Rites as Rituals without any Spirit cost._

_Read more about Relics, Talismans and Rites in_ [_Playing The Game_](../../playing-the-game/)

They are burdened with **Sacred Knowledge** which is theirs to know, and only theirs.

> Sacred Knowledge is owned by the Warden and only they may know their ways. It is used to protect their people and keep them unburdened by the dread things of night.
